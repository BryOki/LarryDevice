The Library

Larry Plays Tiles.

Each tile plays parent-approved content: shows, stories, songs, activities, or family messages.

Kids choose by placing a tile on Larry.

The picture tells them what it plays. The color tells them how long it lasts.

How It Works

Pick a tile. Press play. All done.

1

Kids pick one tile

Tiles contain content like shows, stories, songs, activities, and messages from your children's loved ones.

2

Larry plays it on the TV

Snap the tile onto Larry. Press the big play button.

3

Larry ends the session

Simple controls

Big play. Clear volume. Nothing else to browse.

Light Guide

Larry helps kids choose.

Before a child picks a tile, Larry lights up to show what kind of tile fits.

Kids do not need to read a timer. They just match the tile to the light.

Any tile

If any tile works, Larry shows a soft color blend.

Green

Green means short.

Blue

Blue means medium.

Purple

Purple means longer.

Try another

If a tile does not match the color, Larry gently shows red and asks them to try another.

Parent Control

Simple approval from your phone.

  • Approve new tiles.
  • Set daily limits.
  • Allow a session.
  • See when it ended calmly.

No complicated dashboard. Just the controls parents actually need.

Family Tiles

A tile from the people who love them.

Each child gets their own Family Tile.

Parents, grandparents, and trusted family can send short videos, photos, songs, or stories. Parents preview and approve everything. Then the child places their Family Tile on Larry and presses play.

Show Recaps

Screen time you can talk about.

Larry can send parents a short recap after a show ends: what your child watched, what happened, and a few questions to ask later.

So you can stay connected, even when you were not sitting on the couch.
