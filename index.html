The Product

A simple player for better screen time.

Larry connects to your TV and plays only what parents approve.

How It Works

Pick a tile. Press play. All done.

1

Kids pick one tile

Tiles contain content like shows, stories, games, songs, activities, and messages from your children's loved ones.

2

Larry plays it on the TV

Snap the tile onto Larry. Press the big play button.

3

Larry ends the session

When the tile is done, Larry winds down.

Light Guide

Larry helps kids choose.

Tiles have colored frames. Larry lights up to show which color fits, so kids know which tile to choose.

Try another

If a tile does not match the color, Larry gently shows red and asks them to try another.

Soft Skins

Soft skins. Big personality.

Larry can wear removable silicone skins that add protection, personality, and a little bit of magic.

Parent Control

Simple controls from your phone.

Approve tiles, set daily limits, and allow sessions from one place. No complicated dashboard, just the controls parents actually need.

Family Tiles

A tile from the people who love them.

Each child gets their own Family Tile. Parents, grandparents, and trusted family can send these tiles short videos, photos, songs, or stories children can play with Larry.

Learning Games

Educational games kids can touch.

Larry can turn tiles into simple learning games for letters, numbers, colors, shapes, and matching. The experience is physical, playful, and easy to understand.

Show Recaps

Screen time you can talk about.

Larry can send parents a short recap after a show ends: what your child watched, what happened, and a few questions to ask later.
